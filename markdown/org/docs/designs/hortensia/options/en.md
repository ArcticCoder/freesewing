---
title: "Zipper Size"
---

This option controls allow you to specify the zipper size to be used in the main opening.

Zippers come in pre-defined sizes. These are currently supported:

|Name     |Width in mm|
|Invisible|          0|
|#3       |        4.8|
|#4       |        5.4|
|#4.5     |        5.9|
|#5       |        6.2|
|#6       |          7|
|#8       |          8|
|#10      |         10|
