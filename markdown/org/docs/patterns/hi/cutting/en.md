---
title: "Hi shark plush toy: Cutting Instructions"
---

- **Main fabric (Upper body)**
  - Cut **2 Body** parts.
  - Cut **2 Tail** parts.
  - Cut **2 Top Fin** parts.
  - Cut **2 Bottom Fin** parts.

- **Contrast fabric (Belly)**
  - Cut **1 Belly** part.
  - Cut **2 Above Mouth** parts.

- **Contrast fabric (Mouth)**
  - Cut **1 Mouth** part.

- **Teeth fabric**
  - Cut **1 Upper Teeth** part.
  - Cut **1 Lower Teeth** part.
