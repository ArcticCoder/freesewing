---
title: "Hi the shark: Cutting Instructions"
---

- **Color 1 (traditionally blue)**
  - Cut **2 Body**
  - Cut **2 Tail**
  - Cut **2 Top Fin**
  - Cut **2 Bottom Fin**
- **Color 2 (traditionally white)**
  - Cut **1 Belly**
  - Cut **2 Above Mouth**
  - Cut **1 Upper Teeth**
  - Cut **1 Lower Teeth**
- **Color 3 (traditionally pink)**
  - Cut **1 Mouth**
