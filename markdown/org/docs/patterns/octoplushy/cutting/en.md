---
title: "Octoplushy, the plushy octopus: Cutting Instructions"
---

- **Color 1 (Upper body, top of legs)**
  - Cut **8 head** parts
  - **Octopus only**
    - Cut **2 eyebrow** parts.
  - **Squid only**
    - Cut **2 head (a)** parts
- **Color 2 (Belly, bottom of legs)**
  - Cut **8 leg** parts
  - **Squid only**
    - Cut **2 leg (a)** parts
- **Octopus & Squid (eyes)**
  - Cut **2 eye (white)** parts
  - Cut **2 pupil (black)** parts
